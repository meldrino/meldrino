The problem

Crypto is a mess.
That's not your fault.

Bitcoin lives on one blockchain. Ethereum on another. Nano, Monero, Solana — all completely separate networks that have no idea the others exist. Each needs its own wallet app. Some coins appear in multiple wallets. Some wallets hold multiple coins. Before long you're juggling dozens of apps, all with their own seed phrases, and you still can't answer these simple questions: how much do I actually have and where is it?

The industry's answer has always been "get another wallet." A super-wallet. A multi-chain wallet. But that means trusting yet another app with your keys, moving your crypto, creating yet another account.

Meldrino's answer is different. Don't move anything. Don't trust anyone with your keys. Just finally be able to see everything, in one place.

Getting started

Up and running
in minutes.

01

Install Meldrino

Download from the Google Play Store and install on your Android device. No account required.

02

One-time setup

Meldrino detects which wallet apps you have installed. For each one, you paste in your public address once — it never changes, so you never have to do it again.

03

Connect custodial wallets

For ZBD Lightning, use the Connect page to authenticate securely via QR code. Takes under a minute.

04

See everything

Your full portfolio, consolidated on one screen. Check balances at a glance without switching apps.
