Why Meldrino

Your whole portfolio.
One screen.

🔍

Auto-detects wallets

Meldrino scans your device for installed crypto wallet apps and pulls balances automatically. No manual setup.

🔑

Never touches your keys

Meldrino is read-only. It never asks for seed phrases, private keys, or permissions to move funds. Your crypto stays yours.

⚡

Lightning included

Connect your ZBD wallet to see your Bitcoin Lightning balance alongside everything else. More custodial integrations coming soon.

📊

Consolidated view

Stop switching between five apps to check your balances. Meldrino brings everything into one clean, glanceable dashboard.
